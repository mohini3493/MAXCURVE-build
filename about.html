Our story

MaxCurve originated as a subsidiary of Unitech, a successful UK-based services and product startup founded by Yashwanth Reddy K in 2024. Over time, MaxCurve has established a strong presence across Asia and Europe, empowering some of the world’s leading organizations to transform challenges into sustainable business value, operational excellence, and revenue growth through advanced technology.

With extensive experience, MaxCurve has collaborated with technology firms, Fortune 500 companies, and enterprises of all sizes, delivering impactful solutions across diverse industries such as finance, manufacturing, supply chain, retail, e-commerce, healthcare, and more.

Our Technology Office has undergone significant expansion, serving as a specialized consulting unit offering expertise in Architecture, Cloud, Big Data, AI/ML, IoT, UI/UX, Digital Platforms, and Cybersecurity. In addition, we continue to strengthen our partnerships within the tech ecosystem, working with industry leaders like AWS, GCP, Azure, and Snowflake to shape the future of global industries.

At MaxCurve, we believe that building lasting partnerships is about providing innovative solutions that fuel success and growth for our clients.
